## ğŸŒ Project 1: Global Health Insights Dashboard

### Overview
An interactive Tableau dashboard analysing global health indicators using the **Gapminder Health dataset**. The project explores disparities in life expectancy, population demographics, and health metrics across countries and continents.

### Business Scenario
Designed from the perspective of a **global health organisation**, the dashboard supports rapid understanding of:
- Regional health inequalities
- Long-term life expectancy trends
- Relationships between health indicators

### Key Visualisations
- **Life Expectancy by Continent**  
  Bar chart showing average life expectancy by continent for the most recent year.

- **Life Expectancy Trend (Top 5 Countries)**  
  Line chart tracking life expectancy over time for the top-performing countries.

- **Population Distribution by Gender**  
  Interactive pie chart filtered by country and latest year.

- **Life Expectancy vs BMI**  
  Scatter plot analysing the relationship between average BMI and life expectancy, colour-coded by continent.

- **Additional Insight Visual**  
  Supplementary chart to enhance exploratory analysis.

### Key Insights
- Japan shows the highest life expectancy globally
- Significant variation exists between continents
- Elevated male lung cancer rates in Bulgaria may correlate with smoking prevalence
- Clear relationships emerge between health indicators and outcomes

## ğŸµ Project 2: Spotify Genre & Audio Feature Analysis

### Overview
An exploratory analysis of Spotify track data to uncover trends in **genre popularity and audio characteristics**. The dashboard supports insight generation for music and media organisations.

### Analytical Focus
- Popularity vs musical attributes
- Genre-level comparison across multiple metrics
- Identification of underrepresented but high-performing genres

### Key Metrics Analysed
- Popularity
- Danceability
- Speechiness
- Valence
- Acoustic and energy-related features

### Key Findings
- Top 10 genres by popularity identified
- High danceability and valence do not guarantee popularity
- Genres such as **Reggae and Reggaeton** perform strongly on musical attributes but lag in popularity
- Clear genre clustering across audio features
